I am a Product Manager during the weekdays. I used to be a programmer, but now I just dabble in writing scripts that improve my life. This site is my personal blog and represents only my opinions and hobbies not those of my employer. The blog is here to house my personal projects for role-playing, video games, programming, DIY and cooking. I also like to post notes here about things I learned during my programming experiences at work or with personal projects. I often times write posts on answers to problems that caused me some headaches or time to resolve. For me the resolution is the key and the blog post is there to keep me from repeating the same mistake twice. I have also started up my own Patreon where I post FREE battlemaps for Forgotten Realms. I will link to those posts from this site as well. If you like what I do click the become a patron button below.
